Replace the placeholder YouTube URLs in the portfolio section with your actual video links.

Step 1: Get Your YouTube Video ID

From your YouTube video URL, extract the video ID:

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ

Step 2: Update the HTML

In index.html, find the portfolio section and replace the video URLs:

ðŸ“§ Contact Form Setup

To make the contact form functional, you'll need a backend service. Here are some options:

Option 1: Formspree (Easiest)

Add this to your form tag:

<form class="contact-form glass-card" action="https://formspree.io/f/YOUR_FORM_ID" method="POST">

Option 2: Netlify Forms

Add this to your form tag:

<form class="contact-form glass-card" name="contact" method="POST" data-netlify="true">

Option 3: Custom Backend

Update the form action to point to your server:

<form class="contact-form glass-card" action="/submit-form" method="POST">
